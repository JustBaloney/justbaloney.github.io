About me

Hi, I'm Saloni, Design Lead and Manager at Spotify, London where I’ve co-created product strategy, driven product vision, produced experience frameworks, and managed and coached a team of four early-career to senior designers. I enjoy leading through ambiguity & gnarly problem spaces using insights and deep collaboration. Previous to this, I have worked for over 8 years as a product design IC across a variety of domains & companies from pre-seed startups to fortune 500, collaborating with teams of different sizes & constellations.

Helping people fall in love with Spotify

Spotify · 2023-24

As the design lead for the Activation product area with the mission to "help people fall in love with Spotify", I'm committed to improving in-product journeys to help users unlock value in features, formats & tiers. I drive key business KPIs and opportunities for learning & experimentation across tiers– directly managing & coaching a team of four early-career to senior designers, and overseeing a product area of 5 squads.

A cold start experience that gets you right

Spotify · 2021-22

Led design for cold-start personalisation on the Spotify app, improving activation & retention across formats & markets. Known among the best of personalised onboarding experiences, we personalised the taste onboarding platform itself using machine learning and customized the experience to help diverse users make Spotify theirs.

Helping new users find the right tier on Spotify

Spotify · 2023

As part of a major change to the Free user experience in select markets, I helped reduce conversion losses by improving awareness of Premium for new and free users so as to better inform mental models of the tiers. We tested and shipped nudges to Premium from a broader vision for how we might build conversion journeys across Spotify.

An EV platform for 2 & 3 wheelers

RevOS · 2018

RevOS (now Bolt) was building the first EV platform for 2 & 3 wheelers. I helped the founding team with branding, value chain and UX design during their pre-seed stage when they signed on their first few OEMs and gained seed funding.

Bridging designers' intent & developers' needs

Adobe · 2017

I led design for the 0-1 of the designer-developer collaboration workflows in Adobe XD, collaborating with several other designers. Even in an MVP scope, the solution successfully migrated users' preferred handoff mode from 3rd party apps to within the Adobe eco-system and eased continuous collaboration.

Adobe's automagical features for pro-sumers

Adobe · 2014-15

At Adobe, I helped devise ways to utilise emerging, smart technologies "under-the-hood" to power effortless, "auto-magical" workflows for consumers to edit and organize their media.
