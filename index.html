    Activation for Spotify mobile

    Spotify, 2023-Now

    As a lead for the Activation product area, I drive our mission "to help people fall in love with Spotify". I co-create strategy, evangelize with a design vision and help drive key business KPIs and opportunities for learning & experimentation, managing a team of 4 associate to senior level designers.

    Cold start and taste onboarding for Spotify mobile

    Spotify, 2020-22

    Led design for cold-start personalisation on the Spotify app, which created proven retention gains. Known among the gold standards of personalised onboarding experiences, we personalised the taste onboarding platform itself using machine learning and customized the experience to help diverse users make Spotify theirs.

    First Conversions for Spotify mobile

    Spotify, 2023

    As part of some great changes to the Free user experience, we shipped conversion nudges from a broader exploration across different touchpoints to help create awareness of Premium and inform mental models of the tiers for users coming to the app.

    An EV platform for 2 & 3 wheelers

    RevOS, 2018

    RevOS (now Bolt) was building the first EV platform for 2 & 3 wheelers. I helped the founders with branding, value chain and UX design during their pre-seed stage when they signed on their first few OEMs and gained seed funding.

    Designer-developer collaboration & handoff

    Adobe, 2017

    I led the 0-1 design for designer-developer collaboration workflows in Adobe XD, as part of larger XD team, successfully migrating users' preferred handoff mode away from 3rd party apps and easing continuous collaboration.

    Adobe's automagical features for pro-sumers

    Adobe, 2014-15

    Early in my time at Adobe, I worked on problems which involved taking complex, smart technology and using it to power effortless, "auto-magical" creative workflows in a range of consumer products. It taught me a lot about how to utilize "under-the-hood" technology to shape great experiences.
